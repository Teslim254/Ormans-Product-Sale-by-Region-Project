# Ormans-Product-Sale-by-Region-Project
A business sales analysis dashboard project exploring regional sales performance, product affordability, discount
Product Sales Business Analysis Dashboard
Overview

This project is a comprehensive business intelligence and sales analysis case study developed to evaluate the operational and sales performance of a wholesale retail business across multiple regions, stores, products, and discount structures over a five-year period (2023–2027).

The analysis focuses on uncovering actionable business insights from sales data using data visualization, performance metrics, trend analysis, and regional comparisons. The dashboard was designed to simulate a real-world business reporting environment where management stakeholders can monitor company performance, identify weak areas, and make data-driven decisions.

The project combines:

Business Analysis
Data Visualization
Sales Trend Analysis
KPI Reporting
Discount Impact Evaluation
Regional Performance Assessment
Project Objectives

The primary objectives of this project were to:

Analyze overall sales performance across multiple years
Evaluate regional and store-level business performance
Identify trends in customer purchasing behavior
Assess the impact of discounts on product sales
Examine product return patterns
Compare affordable and expensive product categories
Generate business recommendations from observed trends
Key Business Questions Answered

This analysis attempts to answer important business questions such as:

Which regions perform best in terms of sales and revenue?
Which products generate the highest revenue?
How do discounts influence customer purchasing behavior?
Which products experience the highest return frequency?
Has the business experienced consistent yearly growth?
What relationship exists between affordability and revenue generation?
Which stores perform best under different discount conditions?
Tools & Technologies Used
Microsoft Power BI / Excel – Dashboard development and visualization
Microsoft Excel – Data preparation and cleaning
Business Intelligence Techniques – KPI reporting and trend analysis
Dashboard Features
1. KPI Summary Cards

The dashboard includes key performance indicators such as:

Total Revenue Generated
Number of Sales Made
Number of Sold Items
Returned Items Count

These metrics provide a quick overview of the company's operational performance.

2. Sales Trend Analysis

A time-series analysis was performed to evaluate:

Monthly sales trends
Yearly sales performance
Overall business growth patterns

This section highlights fluctuations in sales performance and helps identify whether the company is improving over time.

3. Product Return Analysis

The project investigates:

Products with the highest return rates
Customer return behavior
Potential quality or satisfaction concerns

The analysis showed that products such as laptops, monitors, tablets, and chairs experienced relatively higher return frequencies compared to others.

4. Affordability vs Revenue Analysis

Products were categorized into:

Affordable items
Expensive items

The dashboard compares:

Sales volume
Revenue contribution
Customer purchasing patterns

One of the major findings revealed that although affordable and expensive products were sold in relatively similar quantities, expensive products contributed a significantly larger percentage of total revenue.

5. Regional Performance Evaluation

The project compares business performance across multiple regions:

Central
East
North
South
West

Regions were evaluated using:

Sales volume
Revenue generated
Customer returns
Returning customer activity

This section helps identify the strongest and weakest performing regions.

6. Discount Impact Analysis

The dashboard explores how different discount rates influence sales performance across:

Regions
Stores
Product categories

The analysis reveals that:

Customers frequently purchase without discounts
Smaller discounts often outperform larger discount rates
Discount effectiveness varies across stores and regions
Major Insights Derived

Some key insights obtained from the analysis include:

The business maintained relatively stable sales over the years but showed limited long-term growth.
Expensive products generated the majority of company revenue despite comparable sales volumes with affordable products.
Some products experienced consistently higher return rates, indicating possible quality or customer satisfaction issues.
The Western region demonstrated stronger overall business performance compared to other regions.
Moderate discount strategies appeared more effective than aggressive discounting in driving sales.
Recommendations

Based on the findings from the analysis, the following recommendations were proposed:

Reevaluate discount strategies to optimize profitability
Investigate high-return products and supplier quality
Increase marketing efforts to improve yearly sales growth
Focus on customer retention and satisfaction
Monitor regional performance continuously to identify underperforming areas
Project Limitations

While the project provides valuable business insights, some limitations exist:

Profit margins were not included in the dataset
Customer demographic analysis was unavailable
Supplier-level data was not provided
The project focuses primarily on sales and operational metrics

Future improvements could include:

Profitability analysis
Forecasting models
Customer segmentation
Predictive analytics
Interactive drill-through dashboards
